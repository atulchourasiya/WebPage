FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS 

NCCHD Financial Management System (FMS) is designed and developed to enable SFURTI Clusters of Council of Handicrafts Development Corporations (NCHHD) to Submit and Manage Pay orders.

You can register for the FMS account of your IA/SPV via this form.

FMS has been launched and utilized by NCHHD to make sure that the pay order and fund disbursement process is done in a standardized, consistent, timely and transparent manner. With FMS, IAs are able to submit supporting document with ease. Additionally, the system itself makes all financial calculations so that there are no calculation related errors.

You can submit the reports by selecting MIS from the sidebar and selecting a report type.

A combination of pay order form and supporting documents are referred as Claim by the system.

You can download the design modules from this page.

Please share the tender advertisement on contact@sfurticohands.com

Upon rejection/approval of a claim from TA and/or NA, a comment is added to clarify the reason of rejection/approval. You can see the comment and submit another claim after fixing the issues. Additionally, a detailed list of common rejection reason per claim type is available for all IAs in the dashboard. To see the list, kindly login to your IA FMS Account and go to Downloads > Common Rejection Reasons.

About NCHHD FMS & MIS

The FMS and MIS 2.0 is a digital platform that simplifies the management of cluster funds for IAs working under NCHHD, featuring various tools such as email support, reporting, and a Management Information System for comprehensive monitoring and decision-making.
Its flexible design allows for future adaptability and scalability.
