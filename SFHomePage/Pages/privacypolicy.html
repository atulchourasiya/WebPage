PRIVACY POLICY 

Last Updated on: April 1st, 2023

This privacy policy explains how NCHHD FMS & MIS (“we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, and protects the personal information of its users (“you” or “your”) when you access or use our website.

What information do we collect?

We collect the following types of personal information from you:

  • IP address: We collect your IP address when you visit our website or app to help us analyze traffic and improve our services.
  • Email address: We collect your email address when you register an account with us or contact us for any inquiries or feedback.
  • Account information: We collect your account information, such as username and password, when you create or update your account with us.
  • Financial information: We collect your financial information, such as bank account details, cluster details, etc.
  • Other information: We may collect other information that you voluntarily provide to us, such as your preferences, interests, feedback, etc.

How do we use your information?

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve our services: We use your personal information to provide and improve our services, such as processing your payorders and applications and responding to your inquiries, etc.
  • To personalize your user experience: We use your personal information to personalize your user experience, such as displaying relevant content, etc., based on your preferences and interests.
  • To send newsletters and updates: We use your email address to send you newsletters and updates about services, events, updates, promotions, etc.
  • To comply with legal obligations: We use your personal information to comply with legal obligations, such as tax laws, anti-money laundering laws, etc.

With whom do we share your information?

We share your personal information with the following third parties:

  • Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics to help us analyze how our users use our website or app. Google Analytics may collect and process your IP address and other information on our behalf. You can learn more about how Google uses your information here: https://policies.google.com/privacy/partners
  • Other third parties: We may share your personal information with other third parties that we work with to provide or improve our services, such as service providers, advertisers, partners, etc. We will only share your personal information with these third parties if they have agreed to protect your personal information in accordance with this privacy policy.

What are your rights and choices?

You have the following rights and choices regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You can request access to your personal information that we hold and receive a copy of it.
  • Update: You can update or correct your personal information that we hold if it is inaccurate or incomplete.
  • Delete: You can request deletion of your personal information that we hold if you no longer want us to use it.
  • Opt out: You can opt out of receiving newsletters and updates from us by clicking the unsubscribe link in the email or contacting us at contact@nchhd.org
  • Withdraw consent: You can withdraw your consent to our use of your personal information at any time by contacting us at contact@nchhd.org

How do we update this policy?

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy on our website or app or by sending you an email. The date of the last update will be indicated at the top of this policy. Your continued use of our website or app after the update will constitute your acceptance of the updated policy.

How can you contact us?

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy or our use of your personal information, please contact us at contact@nchhd.org

About NCHHD FMS & MIS

The FMS and MIS 2.0 is a digital platform that simplifies the management of cluster funds for IAs working under NCHHD, featuring various tools such as email support, reporting, and a Management Information System for comprehensive monitoring and decision-making.
Its flexible design allows for future adaptability and scalability.
